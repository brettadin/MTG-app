# Getting Started - Interactive Tutorial (Overview)

This tutorial will walk you through building your first deck and running a basic playtest simulation. It is a guided list of steps you can follow while using the UI.

## Step 1: Create a New Deck
- Click File -> New Deck or press `Ctrl+N`.
- Choose a name and (optionally) a format.
- The deck panel appears on the right side where your deck will be shown.

## Step 2: Search for Cards
- Type a card name or partial name into the Quick Search bar and press Enter.
- Use the left Search filters to narrow results.
- Click a result to view details in the Card Detail panel.

## Step 3: Add Cards to Your Deck
- In the Card Detail panel, click the `+ Add to Deck` button to add a card to your current deck.
- Repeat until your deck meets the format requirements.

## Step 4: Validate Your Deck
- Click the Validate Deck button (Deck menu) to check for rule violations.
- Fix any errors and revalidate.

## Step 5: Playtest (Goldfish)
- In the Deck menu, choose Playtest or press `Ctrl+T`.
- The simulator will run 1000 hands (or choose a smaller sample) and present statistics about draws, mana curves, and early plays.

## Helpful Hints
- Right-click a result for context menu (where available) to copy name or view more options.
- Use `Ctrl+F` to focus Quick Search.
